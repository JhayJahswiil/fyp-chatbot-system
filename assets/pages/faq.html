FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

üü† What is the Student Project Supervisor Recommendation System?

The Student Project Supervisor Recommendation System is an automated solution designed to match students with suitable project supervisors based on their project topics. It streamlines the supervisor allocation process and enhances the quality of project outcomes.

üü† Who can use the recommendation system?

The recommendation system is currently available for students in the Computer Science Department of Ekiti State University. It is tailored to meet the specific needs of this department.

üü† How does the recommendation system work?

The system utilizes an intelligent algorithm that analyzes the project topics provided by students. It considers factors such as supervisor expertise, availability, and past performance to generate personalized supervisor recommendations.

üü† Can I provide a general topic or does it have to be specific?

It is recommended to provide specific and well-defined project topics to receive accurate supervisor recommendations. Vague or incomplete topics may result in less precise matches.

üü† What if I don't agree with the recommended supervisor?

While the system strives to provide optimal matches, personal preferences and compatibility are subjective factors. If you have concerns or prefer a different supervisor, you can reach out to the department administration to discuss your preferences.

üü† Is the recommendation system available for other departments?

Currently, the system is limited to the Computer Science Department.
